# Site Plan

**Working name:** AI Stack Guide
**Niche:** Reviews and comparisons of AI productivity/software tools for freelancers, solopreneurs, and small teams.
**Why this niche:** SaaS affiliate programs pay much higher commissions than physical goods (often 20-40%, frequently recurring for the life of the subscription), the buyer intent on "best X tool" / "X vs Y" searches is high, and content can be produced entirely from public product info without needing personal experience claims that would be misleading.

**Monetization:** Affiliate links to AI/SaaS tools' official affiliate/partner programs. Placeholder links live in `affiliate-links.json` — you fill in your real affiliate IDs once you're approved for each program (see SETUP.md).

**Initial article set (task #3):**
1. Best AI Writing Assistants for Freelancers in 2026 (Jasper vs Copy.ai vs Grammarly)
2. Best AI Project Management Tools for Small Teams (ClickUp vs Notion vs Asana)
3. Claude vs ChatGPT vs Gemini: Which AI Assistant Should You Use in 2026
4. Best AI SEO Tools to Grow Organic Traffic (Surfer SEO vs Semrush vs Ahrefs)
5. Best AI Video & Presentation Tools (Synthesia vs Descript vs Pictory)
6. 7 AI Tools That Save Freelance Writers 10+ Hours a Week
7. Best All-in-One AI Workspaces for Solopreneurs (Notion AI vs ClickUp AI)
8. How to Start a Side Hustle With AI Tools in 2026 (hub article, links to all above)

**Ongoing content:** `topic-queue.md` holds future article topics. The weekly scheduled task picks the next un-written topic, drafts it, adds affiliate link placeholders, builds the site, and commits.

**Important honesty note:** I picked specific tools/programs based on general knowledge of well-known affiliate programs (Semrush's "BeRush," Jasper, Grammarly, Notion, ClickUp, Surfer SEO, Canva, Descript, Synthesia all have or have had public affiliate programs historically). You must verify each program's current terms, commission rate, and application requirements yourself when you apply — affiliate programs change terms often and I can't apply on your behalf since that requires your identity/tax/payment details.
