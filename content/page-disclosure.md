In accordance with FTC guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, here's how this site is funded.

AI Stack Guide participates in affiliate/partner programs run by the software companies we write about. When you click certain links on this site and sign up for or purchase a product, we may earn a commission. This comes at **no additional cost to you** — pricing is the same whether or not you use our link.

Being an affiliate does not change our recommendations. We aim to describe each tool's actual features, pricing, and limitations accurately based on publicly available information, and we link to alternatives, including tools we do not have affiliate relationships with, when relevant.

If any article includes claims of first-hand testing, that will be stated explicitly in the article itself; otherwise, assume the comparison is based on publicly available documentation and feature comparisons rather than direct hands-on use.
